NEWS

Canalys posted that in the first quarter of 2024, the global personal computer (PC) market showed a healthy growth trend, with total shipments of desktops and laptops increasing by 2.9% year-on-year, reaching 57 million units. The shipment volume of laptops (including mobile workstations) increased by 3.8% to 44.9 million units, while the shipment volume of desktops (including desktop workstations) remained relatively stable, only decreasing by 0.4% to 12.1 million units. Although the growth rate is limited, this indicates that PC demand continues to rebound in various fields, benefiting from the updates of Windows 11 and AI PCs, and the PC market will accelerate growth throughout the year.
