  2. Customers visiting the company's support page or website
  3. The customer first looks at the FAQ to see if there is a direct solution
  4. If the FAQ does not solve the problem, the customer can choose to contact the support team
  5. Customers can choose one of the following contact methods:
    • Chat online
    • Email
    • Telephone
  6. Communicate with the customer and support team to describe the issue
  7. Support team to assist clients in resolving issues
  8. If the issue is not resolved, the customer can choose to continue communication or seek other support channels
